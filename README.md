## Goals
Target Audience:
Zillenials

Persona:
Daisy wants is a zillenial. It is year 2025 and in 2026, she is graduating and intends on finding a job. She also wants to save up money so she move out in 2027. She has too many goals and she is tired of making a schedule every year, week, and everyday. It is exhausting and it really gets to her.

Competitor:
UseMotion: In essence, Motion serves as an AI personal assistant that handles much  of the logistical "busywork" of managing time and projects, allowing  individuals and teams to focus on actual work and strategic  decision-making.
Our app: personal assistant that assists the user by creating a schedule and saves the user hours.


## UserStories
- Takes too long to make a schedule, especially when you have to try and put all the tasks on a calendar.  → Enter all tasks into the form and it generates a schedule for you.
- Digital schedule means going on your phone and going on the app takes time. It may be hard to follow.    → Calendar schedule layout so user can clearly see all their tasks.
- Making a physical schedule can be hard to follow, especially if you forget it at home.                   → Remains on your phone all day, user can add a square on their home screen as well.
- Forgetting about the schedule, especially when the day gets busy.                                        → Reminders through notifications
- Having too many tasks can leave user feeling overwhelmed, having support is nice.                        → Encouraging quotes / gentle reminder to check off the tasks you completed, gentle reminders to complete a task if you haven’t started / calming                                                                                                              activities / gamified                                                                                                                           - Committing to big goals can be tough, especially when we get busy and life gets in the way               → User enters goals to accomplish for the year, it is listed on the main screen so user commits to it / Goals can be broken down so user can                                                                                                                   follow it easily / Can be used on computer, ipad, and phones
- Having too many goals is possible but it can seem too cluttered, thus making it hard to complete         → Categorizes goals so user has a clear image of what needs to be done.
- Too many words alone can be hard to follow and make the goal seem boring                                 → User can upload images to make their goals more readable.
- Working on goals can feel lonely and self validation can be hard to follow through.                      → User can keep a streak going, streak is valid if user completes the top three tasks of the day / completing more than three tasks means they                                                                                                                 get a heart that fills a cup / If they miss a day of recording, user can use a heart so they dont lose their streak / user gets to pick if they                                                                                                              want energy or a heart for the cup)
